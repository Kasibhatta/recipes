Ingredients:
 - 200 gm paneer
 - 4 large tomatoes chopped
 - 2 large onions - chopped
 - 50gm cashew nuts
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 tablespoon garam masala
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 100gm butter
 - 
Directions:
 1. Add Tomatoes, onions, cashee nuts into a blender and crush them to nice paste.
 2. Put butter into a pan and heat it up for a minute and then add the paste we made.
 3. Add all the garam masala items to it.
 4. Put a lid on the pan and let the items be cooked for 8-10 minutes in low-medium heat.
 5. Add salt and paneer cubes to this.
 6. Let the items cook for another 2-3 minutes.  You are ready
